Backstory

Born and raised in the Chicago suburbs. Ever since my parents first got me a Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) when I was a little kid I have been hooked on games. Growing up I played a lot of Nintendo games and the MMO MapleStory. Nowadays I love to play all sorts of games from RPGs to FPS. When I'm designing you might see a lot of my references and inspiration come from games. Gaming also fuels my competitive side where I strive to improve myself and climb the ranks. By playing competitive team games online I have been able to improve my communication skills, collaboration, and being receptive to feedback to help improve my gameplay.

I have always been a very creative person, whenever I saw the opportunity in school I would sign up for art classes and draw in my free time. Maybe that's why my favorite part of any UX project is visioning, storyboarding, and creating mockups. One of my favorite creative mediums has to be painting, whether with acrylics or digitally. In an art museum I would definitely have to stop by the Impressionist gallery. Games definitely drive and inspire many of my creative pursuits. I have tried to learn piano to be able to play songs from Kingdom Hearts before and learned how to sew so I could make things like large squid plushies from Splatoon. Always curious and eager to learn about new things when inspiration hits.
