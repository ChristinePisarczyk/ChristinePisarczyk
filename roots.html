Side Quest

objective

Develop a kitchen experience that enables people of different generations to cook and eat food, engage, entertain, and enjoy being together

description

An interaction design course project focused on how we could design for preserving family recipes with each generation. We were motivated by how food brings people together and especially how family recipes are capable of provoking nostalgia of one's family and childhood.

party size

3 members

Duration

4 months

Role

Interaction Designer

Rewards

+3000 xp
+8 Interaction design
+5 User Research
+3 Story telling
+4 Prototyping
+2 Video Editing

Happiness is homemade

This was a group project in our interaction design studio. As a group we were presented with a design brief and we were inspired to focus on designing an experience focused on family recipes. We conducted mixed methods research to learn more about our users, created paper prototypes and evaluated them. Our final deliverable included a hi-fi prototype and video demonstrating how our product could exist in the real world. You can learn more about this project by reading our article covering our entire design process!
